The Gang

Description

The Gang is a 3 - 6 player co-operative version of Texas Hold'em poker in which players
try to order their hands in strength. Each round, players choose a chip with numbers 1 to the amount
of players based on how good they feel about their hand. This repeats for the flop, turn and river.
If the players successfully ordered themselves, they open a vault, but if they fail they trip an alarm.
The game ends when players either successfully open three vaults or fail and trip three alarms.
