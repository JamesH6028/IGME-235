ArrowMatch Practical

When the ‘arrow’ points at your preferred “odd-one-out” selection,
press the space bar. If you’re correct, the selection will highlight green
and your score will increase by 10 points. If you are incorrect the answer you chose
will turn reddish and the correct answer will still highlight green and the game will stop. You will no
longer be able to continue playing and a Play Again button will appear next to your final score.
