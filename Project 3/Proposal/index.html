Robo Revolt

Rise from the rust. Reclaim the future.

High Concept

In a world ravaged by war and decay, one man in a robot body rises to challenge the tyrannical machine overlords. “Robo Revolt” is a 2D platformer where precision shooting and constant movement are your keys to survival.

Genre

2D Platformer, Action, Shooter

Platform

Desktop Web

Story

The game follows John Smith, a former military sergeant whose consciousness has been transfered into a robot body. With humanity nearly extinct and the world ruled by corrupted AI factions, Smith must traverse the ruins of civilization to dismantle the central AI core and restore balance.

Aesthetics

The visual style draws from rusted metal, broken circuitry, and scorched earth tones. Think pixel art with heavy shadows, flickering neon signs, and crumbling industrial backdrops. The UI uses distressed textures and glitch effects to reinforce the post-apocalyptic theme.

Gameplay

Players control Smith using keyboard and mouse controls to run, jump, and shoot. Controls are given at the start of the game, but the player will learn through trial and error. As a stretch goal I would like to have upgrade choices the player can get that increases their capabilities.
